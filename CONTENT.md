## Who We Are (`#who-we-are`)

**Heading:** A hybrid institute, built over cups of tea and coffee.

**Body:** Condensed origin story — a few of us, young, middle-aged, and old, sat over tea and coffee before zeroing in on the idea of a forward-looking, pioneering institution for leadership excellence, institutional capacity, and research, training, and innovation, in India and internationally. DevSolve is a global institute in evolution, providing development solutions through knowledge, skills, and advanced technologies.

**Hybrid model (4 pillars):**

1. Research & Knowledge — evidence-based policy analysis and action research.
2. Training & Capacity Building — online, offline, and blended learning with handholding.
3. Technology & Innovation — AI, data systems, and digital tools for governance.
4. Advisory & Consulting — consultancy support for governments and organisations.
